1. Roasted Garlic Broccoli (15 min)

Ingredients: Broccoli florets, olive oil, garlic, salt, red pepper flakes.

  1. Toss broccoli + oil + garlic + salt + pepper flakes. Roast at 220°C/425°F for 12–15 minutes.
  2. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and a shower of parmesan (optional).

2. Sweet Potato & Black Bean Bowl (25 min)

Ingredients: Sweet potato, canned black beans, lime, cumin, greens, avocado.

  1. Roast cubed sweet potato with oil, salt, and cumin for 20 minutes.
  2. Warm black beans, toss with lime. Serve over greens with avocado.

3. Zesty Green Smoothie (5 min)

Ingredients: Spinach, frozen banana, yogurt or plant milk, honey, chia seeds.

  1. Blend everything until silky. Drink like you own the day.

Tasty, fast, and zero judgement.
